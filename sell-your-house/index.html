We want to help

Finding a buyer may not be very hard. Finding a qualified buyer may be a completely different story, Either way, Getting a mortgage can be tough even for the qualified buyer. Statistically, 80% of Americans cannot walk to into a bank today and qualify for a loan, and most people can’t just put down 20% in cash for a down payment. This causes even the most iron-clad offers to disintegrate last minute, over-and-over due to funding issues. regardless, selling a house in today’s market can an uphill battle.

Our qualification process

Research

First, Our qualification requirements for our buyers is extensive and detailed. After personally verifying qualifications we also employ the use of a third-party company who verifies job, income, credit, credit score and credit history, debt-to-income ratios, credit/mortgage readiness, as well as a criminal background check. Their reports provide us with the specific reasons the buyer cannot finance today and give us a time-frame and plan to make the buyer a better qualified candidate. Every buyer is vetted thoroughly, and you can choose who would like to work with.

Create Terms

Second, is our ability to structure favorable terms and conditions. From the information provided to us by the third-party company, we can custom-tailor the terms around the buyer’s ability and time-frame to finance, while at the same time meeting the seller’s needs and requirements. Conditions to be considered are the purchase price, monthly payment, length of term and downpayment.

Make Deal

The third and final step of our process is how we implement and execute the final agreement. Our team does everything in their power to make sure we are creating a successful purchase for our buyer, our success is ultimately confirmed by our buyer obtaining a mortgage. We work with them closely throughout their term with us to ensure their success in becoming credit worthy and approved for a mortgage
